Apply for a Dartford Crossing Local Resident Discount Scheme

This account acts as a personal manual top-up account until the documents are verified and approved.
Once approved the account will be updated to an LRDS account.

Use this service to pay for an annual payment that allows you to travel across the crossing as many times as you want to each year. You will need to verify your details annually

You must:

  • provide evidence by uploading the documents. Alternatively you can send them to us by post.
  • Add all vehicles registered at the address
  • Pay for crossings whilst we are checking your documents

You will need to provide:

  • Your vehicle details
  • A current tax bill that matches the address in your application
  • Proof of your vehicle registered to the address in the application, e.g a copy of your V5C vehicle registration or vehicle lease in your name
  • A debit or credit card

Other ways to pay

You can:

  • pay with cash at your nearest Payzone store
  • set up an account by post
  • pay in advance or top-up your account by post
  • pay or set up an account by phone
